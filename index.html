Features

1. Collection of users information around the state while they are registering. Record the information for further communication for multiple purpose including survey.

2. Provide information regarding metro rail like tracking metros, viewing routes and directions, recharging their metro cards, calculating fare, etc.

3. Provide information to admin of most recent technology can be used for monitoring/updating data of customers. By doing this admin can get their work done in minutes or seconds rather than manually doing it for hours.

About Us

Providing the most actionable App

This project is focusing the preparation of a monitoring tool, which intends to provide information about Metro Rail.

For unit-level information about metro rails, MRMT is a great, user friendly tool that's easy to configure and set up.

Our Mission

The objective of this project is to provide facility of online information to user of Delhi metro who need to know about metro routes/gates, recharge process of their metro cards, metro rail timings etc.
